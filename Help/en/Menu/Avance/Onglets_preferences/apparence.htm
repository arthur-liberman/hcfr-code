Appearence...
 

This box allows you to personalize the style of posting of the application HCFR Colorimeter. It is possible to modify the style of colors employed.
 


 

While clicking on “Appearance”, the “Preference” dialog box appears like this.
 

 
 

The options of this box do not modify any of the calculation results of the application.
 

The graphs background can be either black or white. By default, they are black on screen (to avoid light emitted by control monitor change probe measures), and white in saved files (to avoid consuming ink when printing images).
